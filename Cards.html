Cloud Computing

Cloud computing is the on-
demand availability of
computer system resources,
especially datastorage and
computing power,without direct
active management by the user.
The term is generally used to
describe datacenters available
to many users over the Internet.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the
simulation of human intelligence
processes by machines, especially
computer systems. These processes
include learning (the acquisition
of information and rules for using
the information), reasoning (using
rules to reach approximate ordefinite
conclusions) and self-correction.

Blockchain

A blockchain is a digital record of
transactions. The name comes from
its structure, in which individual
records, called blocks, are linked
together in single list, called a
chain. Blockchains are used for
recording transactions made with
cryptocurrencies, such as Bitcoin,
and have many other applications.
